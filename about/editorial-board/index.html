Editorial Advisory Board

The ToneQuest Report is guided by some of the most respected voices in the guitar world.

Our advisory board brings together builders, performers, scholars, and specialists whose combined knowledge spans every dimension of the guitar world. Their counsel informs our editorial direction and ensures that ToneQuest remains grounded in the deepest expertise available.

TW

Thomas Whitfield

Master Luthier & Guitar Historian

38-year career building custom acoustic and electric instruments. Former instructor at the Roberto-Vega Guitar Institute. Author of The Grammar of the Electric Guitar (2018). His archival work on early American lutherie has informed the collector and restoration communities alike.

MD

Marina Delgado

Session Guitarist & Recording Artist

Nashville and Los Angeles session guitarist with credits on over 400 albums across country, Americana, and roots rock. Adjunct professor of guitar performance at Belmont University. Her recordings are widely cited as benchmarks of clean tone and tasteful phrasing.

RK

Robert Kessler

Amplifier Designer & Technician

Founder of Kessler Amplification. 30 years of boutique amp design. Former chief technician for Fender Custom Shop. Known for his research into original tweed-era circuit topography and the practical implications of component drift on vintage amp character.

YT

Yuko Tanaka

Vintage Guitar Specialist & Appraiser

Principal appraiser at Tanaka Vintage Guitar, Tokyo and Los Angeles. Contributor to the Official Vintage Guitar Price Guide for twelve years. Specializes in pre-1965 American electrics and has authenticated instruments for several major institutional collections.

ED

Earl "Preacher" Dupree

Blues Guitarist & Oral Historian

Baton Rouge-born guitarist with 50 years of performing experience. Living repository of Delta and swamp blues guitar techniques. Subject of the 2021 documentary Preacher's Blues. His workshops on regional guitar traditions are conducted annually at institutions across the American South.

SK

Sandra Kovacs

Pickup Designer & Electronics Engineer

Electrical engineer turned boutique pickup winder. Founded Kovacs Magnetic Designs in 2009. Research focus: Alnico alloy variations and their effect on pickup resonance and harmonic content. Her technical writing has appeared in peer-reviewed journals on acoustic transducers.

The Editorial Advisory Board serves in an advisory capacity. Board members do not receive compensation and have no involvement in the day-to-day editorial process. ToneQuest's independence remains absolute.
